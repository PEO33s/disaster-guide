ℹ️ About This App

This app offers clear, trusted guidance for three major hazards: Earthquakes, Typhoons, and Landslides. Discover what they are, key historical events, essential do’s & don’ts, preparedness tips, and emergency contacts.

Features include interactive checklists, hotlines, and a Shake-to-Alert sound trigger. Requires an internet connection. This is a guide only—not a substitute for emergency services.

✅ Capabilities

  • Accurate info on Earthquakes, Typhoons, Landslides
  • Safety tips and preparedness checklists
  • Emergency hotlines and contact info
  • Shake-to-Alert sound feature

⚠️ Limitations

  • Focuses on only three hazards
  • Requires internet access
  • Informational only—not a live emergency tool
